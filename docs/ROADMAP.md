>
> **How to update.** When a layer completes, change its status and add a one-line note. When a layer starts, mark it `in progress`. Don't write a diary ‚Äî keep it terse.
>
> **Audience.** Anyone (human or AI) opening a fresh chat on this repo. Read this first to know what to skip and what to pick up.
